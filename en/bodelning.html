Division of Assets - We guide you through the process

During divorce or separation, assets and debts are divided through asset division, which may include properties, bank accounts, pensions and other assets. Cohabiting partners may also have rights to certain property upon separation, although the rules are more limited.

Why hire a lawyer?

Asset division can be both legally complex and emotionally challenging. An experienced lawyer ensures your rights are protected and helps you achieve a fair solution.
